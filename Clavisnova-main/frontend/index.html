Our Mission

ClavisNova is a volunteer-led initiative founded by musicians and educators. We believe quality instruments should not gather dust while schools lack arts funding.

By moving pianos from donors to classrooms, we open doors to creativity, discipline, and joy for students—while our free concert recording program celebrates young performers across Connecticut, New Jersey, and New York.

Piano Donation

Universities, educational institutions, and venues frequently retire older pianos. We rescue, inspect, and donate them exclusively to under-resourced schools, so students have access to quality instruments.
